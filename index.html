Prevention from Corona Virus

stay home, stay safe

how to prevent

how to prevent virus

wash your place

clean and disinfect surfaces regularly.

maintain distance

stay at least one meter away from people, and even further when indoors.

don't touch face

avoid touching your face, especially your eyes, nose and mouth.

wash your hand

wash your hands regularly or use alchol-based hand sanitiser.

use napkin

cough or sneeze into napkin or your elbow and wash your napkin and hands after.

wear a mask

wear a face mask around others and also in crowded area.

covid-19 symptoms

dry cough

sore throat

cold

fever

headache

vomiting

take precautions against covid-19

things you should DO

wash your hand

wash hands often. use soap and water or an alcohol-based hand rub.

always wear a mask

always wear a mask in public places, crowded area and infront of other peoples.

stay home during fever

stay at home during fever. stay isolated if you feel unwell.

things you should NOT DO

avoid close contact with animals

if you go to the market or in the home, don't touch animals or anything in the area they stay.

avoid close contact with peoples

avoid confined or involve close contact with others.

avoid crowded area

avoid places that are crowded,confined or involve close contact with others,especially indoors.
